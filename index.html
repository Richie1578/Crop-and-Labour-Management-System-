App Information

FarmTrack Zambia is a specialized crop and labour management system designed to empower farmers with data-driven insights. From GPS field mapping to AI-powered crop advice, we provide the tools needed for modern agriculture.

Version: 1.2.0 (Stable)
Release: Zambia 2026/2027 Season
Policies & Legal

Privacy Policy

We value your farm's data privacy. Your data is stored securely in encrypted cloud databases. We do not share individual farm performance data with third parties. Usage analytics are used solely to improve system performance.

Terms of Service

By using FarmTrack, you agree to provide accurate records for system integrity. AI advice is for guidance only; final agricultural decisions should be verified by a qualified agronomist in your specific district.
